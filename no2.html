Description

Book-1:
It Starts With Us:

"It Starts with Us" is a compelling novel that delves into intricate human emotions, relationships, and life-altering decisions. Through its well-crafted characters and unforeseen twists, the book emphasizes the profound impact of individual choices on our lives and the lives of those we care about."

Book-2:
The Four Agreements:

"The Four Agreements" by Don Miguel Ruiz distills ancient Toltec wisdom into four simple but profound principles: Be Impeccable with Your Word, Don't Take Anything Personally, Don't Make Assumptions, and Always Do Your Best.

Book-3:
The Things You Can See Only When You Slow Down:

"The Things You Can See Only When You Slow Down" by Haemin Sunim is a meditative book that reminds us to embrace mindfulness in our busy lives. Through its tranquil prose, it encourages appreciating life's simple moments and finding solace in stillness.

Book-4:
It Ends With Us:

"It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover is a poignant novel that delves into the intricacies of love, strength, and overcoming challenges. Through its characters' journey, the book tackles tough subjects like domestic abuse, offering a message of empowerment and growth.
